Link Shorteners¶

Lists of known link shorteners recognized by pewtils.http utility methods such as pewtils.http.canonical_link() and pewtils.http.extract_domain_from_url(). These lists were compiled from several collections of shortened links found in social media posts and news articles, so most of the shorteners belong to news outlets and large popular websites, especially those with political content. Since domains can be retired or may change ownership and get redirected to different websites over time, these lists may not be perfectly accurate. We will try to keep them updated as we become aware of changes, but if you notice any inaccuracies or wish to add to these lists, please consider making a pull request!
